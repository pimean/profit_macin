Introduction

With our Profit Margin Calculator, you can quickly and easily calculate the Net Profit and Net Profit Margin for your business.

Whether you're making, trading products, or doing business in general, simply input two of the following values - Cost and Sale Revenue - and we'll calculate the Net Profit and Profit Margin.

  • Cost: The cost of the product.
  • Sale Revenue: The revenue generated by selling the product.
  • Net Profit: The money amount gross profit of the product.
  • Net Profit Margin: The percentage gross profit of the product vs. revenue.

What is Margin

In finance, the term "margin" can refer to different things depending on the context.

For businesses, it usually refers to the amount by which revenue from sales exceeds costs, expressed as a percentage.

This is known as the profit margin, which can also be calculated as net income divided by revenue or net profit divided by sales.

The higher the profit margin, the better, as it indicates that the business is earning more than it is spending.
